Our Mission:

People live their everyday lives oblivious that every decision they make alters their impact on our environment. EcoTraQ is a carbon footprint calculator that helps people understand and reduce their environmental impact, it is a tool for tracking personal emissions and website carbon footprints. Users input their lifestyle choices or website links to see how much carbon they release. By comparing userâ€™s results with the average emissions released per capita, we can better understand how to live a more efficient lifestyle. Our project aims to raise awareness for individuals to take action towards a more sustainable future.

Questionnaire:

Estimation

Before starting the questionaire, estimate what you think your carbon footprint is. (**In tonnes**)

Transportation

1. How do you typically commute to school or work?

2. If you answered car or bus - How long does it take you to commute to work/school

3. How often do you take flights for leisure or business?


Home

4. How do you heat your home?

6. Do you use any of the following? Dryer, washer, dishwasher, or refridgerator


Food

7. What best describes your diet?

8. (optional) How often do you consume meat or dairy products?


Habits

9.How long do you shower (no bath)

10.Do you buy locally-produced goods?

11.Do you recycle?

12. How often do you leave lights on when not in use?
